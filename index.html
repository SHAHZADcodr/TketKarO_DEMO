    IRCTC Train Ticket Booking Online

    Here’s how to book IRCTC tickets online:

    • Open the TketKarO website or download mobile app. Click on book train tickets section.
    • Select the source and destination Indian Railway stations from the list.
    • Pick a date of departure and travel class-AC First Class, Sleeper Class, Second AC, Third AC, AC Chair car.
    • Choose your Indian Railway train and travel class based on arrivel time and travel time (in hours).
    • Fill in the passenger details including berth perference, names of children, senior citizens.
    • Log in using your IRCTC user ID and password. Create a new IRCTC username if you don't have one.
    • Complete the payment and reservation process using your IRCTC login details. You will be redirected to IRCTC website.
    • You will then receive a confirmation on your registered email id and SMS on mobile number.

    IRCTC Login

    On TketKarO, you can register on IRCTC, navigate IRCTC’s next-generation login process, reset your IRCTC password and more.

    Create Your IRCTC Login ID and Password

    Having an IRCTC id and password is the first step towards your booking process. Create IRCTC login ID and password by following the below steps:

    • Open the ixigo trains app.
    • Tap on the profile icon at the bottom right of the page.
    • Now, tap on 'Link your IRCTC Account.'
    • You will see 'IRCTC username.' Tap on it.
    • A pop-up will appear. Tap on 'Register' located at the bottom right.
    • Now, fill up the required details and proceed to get an OTP.
    • Please enter the OTP and verify your email ID and mobile number.
    • Your IRCTC account will be successfully created

    Recover Your IRCTC Login ID

    You can easily recover your IRCTC login ID by following the below steps:

    • Open the ixigo trains app.
    • Tap on the profile icon at the bottom right of the page.
    • Now, tap on 'Link your IRCTC Account.'
    • Then, tap on 'Forgot your IRCTC User ID'.
    • Enter your IRCTC registered Email ID or mobile number and proceed.

    Register as a New User on IRCTC

    Here are the steps to register as a new user on IRCTC:

    • Open the TketKarO trains app.
    • Tap on the profile icon at the bottom right of the page.
    • Now, tap on 'Link your IRCTC Account.'
    • Tap on 'Register on IRCTC'.
    • Enter your details and proceed.
